Who we are

We are a group of friends who took charge of their dreams and want to share their art with the world, art that is expressed through video games. In them, we pour all our energy, knowledge and care. Our desire is to reach the hearts of all those who know us, with incredible and unforgettable stories and unlimited entertainment.

We are entrepreneurs who give everything we have to leave our mark on each of our works.

EVA play 2020 awards

We participated in the EVA Play 2020 presenting our first project: Septem, an adventure game, within the program "My first published video game". After being seen and tested by a jury we were awarded 3rd place in this category.

"My first published video game", or just MPVP, is a program provided by ADVA to promote indie developers.
