Our beginig

Carayá is an entrepreneurship originated in Misiones, Argentina. Our growth was fast, with the help of people with the will to grow the community of Argentine video game developers, associations like ADVA (Argentine Video Game Developers Association), and a vast group of wonderful people. We chase our dreams and we thank everyone who helped us make it possible.

Our philosophy

Strong footsteps

From day one our first footfall was strong and loud, and we intend to keep it that way. This is a characteristic of the Carayá, a noisy animal of our region, from whom we took our name.

Human bonds

We care about our community and maintaining the bond between us. After all, we are a group of friends and we intend to maintain that same spirit of friendship as we grow.
